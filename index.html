Model Weights

This score is a decision aid. Use it to rank targets, then check lineups, weather, park, and market price.

Weights

How to feed it

KHRUse K% with HR/PA. Lower strikeout rate plus real HR rate is better than chasing only raw homers.
ISOUse FanGraphs or Baseball Savant season/split ISO. High ISO is the backbone of the hitter power score.
Pitch fitGrade 0-100 from batter run value or slugging against the pitcher’s main pitch mix.
Last 10Small sample. Useful context, but capped so it cannot dominate the board.
PitcherHR/9 and barrel rate allowed expose mistake-prone arms and bad contact profiles.
ContextPark and weather are global boosts. Confirm lineups before trusting any rank.
